ABOUT US

Founded in 2017, the Zimbabwe Australia Business Council is a not-for-profit, non-partisan organization representing business leaders in every region and sector of Zimbabwe. The Council’s member companies currently employ 1.7 million Zimbabewans and include thousands of businesses of all sizes.

Invest in

zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is home to a well educated and eager workforce. The country is rich, not just in human capital, but also in mineral resources, arable land, and an emerging technology sector. With a young, growing population, Zimbabwe is ripe for investment.

If you’re looking to invest in Zimbabwean businesses, leave your information and we’ll connect you with great opportunities.
