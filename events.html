Mining Forum

The Mining Forum held on April 4th at the Leopard Rock Hotel was a huge success, and a first for the Zimbabwe Australia Business Council.

Much was discussed on Government Policy and key stakeholders from the Industry of Mines were present. Overall, a great conversation was had and the propsects for a resurgence in the Zimbabwean mining sector are looking as good as ever.

You won’t want to miss the next forum.
